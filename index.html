Stephen J Eglen


I am a Reader in Computational Neuroscience in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics, and member of the Cambridge Computational Biology Institute CCBI. I use computational techniques to investigate development of the nervous system. I am also director of the MPhil in Computational Biology. Fellow of Magdalene College.
