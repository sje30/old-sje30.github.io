Resources


Here you can find various resources that I have made available to the community. A key principle of our work is that whenever possible, we make our code and data freely available to the community. You can find links to the relevant code/data alongside each paper listed on my publications page.

In addition to those papers, I highlight some key resources below.

IntactEye

IntactEye is a program for measuring the location of injection sites in the intact eye.

SA3D

R

I’ve written a short article in PLoS Computational Biology describing my experiences teaching R to graduate students. Lecture notes (in PDF) were provided as supplementary information. Together with my colleague Laurent Gatto, we have updated the material.

I develop and maintain several R packages for my research. The most popular package is probably sjemea for analysis of multielectrode array data.
